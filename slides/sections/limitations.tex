\section{Limitations}

\begin{frame}{Current Limitations}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item CMAPSS and IMS do not cover every real deployment condition.
    \item IMS labels are implicit and require careful preprocessing.
    \item The current scaffold leaves room for calibration and interpretability work.
    \item Maintenance action labels may need hand-designed heuristics at first.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
